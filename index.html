Tukio

Tukio is an app that allows authenticated users to search event tickets through Eventful API, save events of their choice and commit to buy discounted tickets for those events. A progress bar dynamically updates as users commit to buy tickets for the events.

My Role:

Google Maps API + Eventful API. Using Reactjs+Redux I was responsible for taking the users Search Results from the Eventful API and displaying them as markers on the map which represented the various venues from their related search.

City Trek

City Trek is an application that creates complete destination planning between multiple users with the ability to share and highlight different real-time data to customize, share, and enhance the end-user's travel experience throughout any city in the United States.

My Role:

MapBox API + Leaflet API. I was responsible for creating a customized map, purely designed for User Experience (UX), and link it to the various data sets given from the Leaflet API to provide a custom map with directions for walking, biking, and driving throughout any city with the proper search parameters.

North East Trees

North East Trees is a community based, grassroots, environmental non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. They were the first design build non-profit organization in Los Angeles. North East Trees’ staff includes licensed professional arborists, landscape architects and landscape designers who ensure efficient and effective end-to end project management.

NET has worked in degraded areas and derelict parcels of land and has successfully rehabilitated, enhanced and beautified them for the benefit of community members. Additionally, these areas have been transformed from blighted and unsafe to inviting and healthful ones that provide improved air quality, and stormwater infiltration, reduction of the heat island effect, and a healthier environment.

My Role:

Volunteered with the Environmental Coordinator to redesign and develop their website and online presence from scratch to produce new ways for environmental awareness, community outreach, and urban planning.
